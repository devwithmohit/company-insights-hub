## Important Notes & Disclaimers

1. **Salary Ranges:** All salary figures are approximate estimates based on available data from Glassdoor, Indeed, PayScale, and employee reviews. Actual salaries may vary based on skills, negotiation, location, and company policies.

2. **Work Mode:** Post-pandemic, many companies have flexible or evolving work-from-home policies. Always confirm current policies during interviews.

3. **Company Information:** Information is based on publicly available data as of November 2025. Company policies, sizes, and benefits may change.

4. **Limited Data Companies (8-10):** For companies with limited public information, exercise additional caution:
   - Ask detailed questions during interviews
   - Request to speak with current employees
   - Check multiple review platforms
   - Understand growth opportunities clearly
   - Get all commitments in writing

5. **Verification:** Always verify all details directly with the company during your interview process, especially regarding:
   - Exact CTC breakup (fixed vs. variable)
   - Bond periods and terms
   - Work mode and flexibility
   - Career growth paths
   - Projects and technologies you'll work on

6. **Reviews:** Company reviews can be subjective. Look for patterns across multiple reviews rather than single opinions.

---

**Research Recommendation:** Before accepting any offer, thoroughly research the company, speak with current/former employees if possible, and ensure all terms are clearly documented in your offer letter.
